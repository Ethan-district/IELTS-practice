IELTS Practice Resources

Here are some reputable websites offering free IELTS practice materials for listening, reading, writing, and speaking:

  • British Council: Provides official IELTS practice tests with answers.
  • IELTS-Up: Offers a collection of free IELTS listening and reading practice tests.
  • IELTS Buddy: Features online IELTS listening and writing tests for practice.
  • BestMyTest: Provides over 125 IELTS listening practice tests with answers.
  • IELTS Online Tests: Offers free online IELTS practice tests for all sections.
  • Exam English: Provides free practice tests for IELTS listening, reading, and speaking.
  • IELTS Liz: Offers tips, lessons, and practice materials for all parts of the IELTS test.
  • IELTS Practice Online: Includes practice questions and tests for all four sections of IELTS.
